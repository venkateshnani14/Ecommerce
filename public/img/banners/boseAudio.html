FAQS

Will noise cancelling earbuds stay in my ears during workouts?

Yes. Our noise cancelling earbuds come with a variety of eartips that are designed to stay securely in your ears during workouts and other physical activities.

Are Bose headphones and earbuds comfortable to wear for long periods of time?

Yes. Comfort is a top priority for us at Bose, and we design our headphones and earbuds to be comfortable for extended wear. Many of our models come with soft, padded cushions or eartips that conform to your ears.

Can Bose earbuds be used for phone calls?

Yes. All Bose earbuds have built-in microphones and can be used for phone calls with clear audio quality.

What is the difference between Bose earbuds and over-ear headphones?

Bose earbuds are smaller and more portable. While over-ear headphones can provide slightly improved noise isolation, earbuds are less bulky and are ideal for a range of daily activities and routines.

How do I pair my Bose earbuds with my smartphone or tablet?

To pair Bose earbuds with a smartphone or tablet, you will need to turn on the earbuds and then put them in pairing mode. Once in pairing mode, your device can then search for the earbuds and connect to them.
